John Elder is Professor of Finance at Colorado State University. My research can be found at Google Scholar. I have served in editorial roles at Journal of Financial Research (Associate Editor), Journal of Economics and Finance (Editor-in-Chief) and Empirical Economics, and for more than 20 years have worked with large investment managers, credentialing organizations, and other clients. I have held administrative posts such as associate dean and department chair, received several faculty fellowships (EKS&H, FirstBank, Kemble Family, Daniels Fund), and been honored with awards for teaching, service, research, and editorial work. I completed my PhD in financial economics at the University of Virginia and hold the CFA charter and FRM designation.
