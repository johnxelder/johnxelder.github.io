About
John Elder is Professor of Finance at Colorado State University. My research can be found at Google Scholar. I have served in editorial roles at Journal of Financial Research (Associate Editor), Journal of Economics and Finance (Editor-in-Chief), and Empirical Economics. For more than 20 years, I have worked with large investment managers, credentialing organizations, and other clients. I have previously held administrative positions such as associate dean and department chair; several faculty fellowships (EKS&H, Firstbank, Kemble Family, Daniels Fund); and am honored to have received several awards for teaching, service, research, and editorial service. I completed my PhD with a field in financial economics at the University of Virginia and have held two professional designations: the CFA charter and the FRM designation.
